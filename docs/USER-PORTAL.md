## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

To start using the User Portal, you'll need to create an account:

1. Go to the **Sign Up** page
2. Enter your **email address**
3. Select your **date of birth** (you must be at least 18 years old)
4. Create a **password** (minimum 6 characters)
5. **Confirm your password** by typing it again
6. Click the **Sign Up** button

After signing up, you'll receive a **verification email**. Click the link in that email to activate your account, then you can log in.

### Logging In

Once your account is created and verified:

1. Go to the **Login** page
2. Enter your **email address**
3. Enter your **password**
4. Click the **Sign In** button

You'll be taken directly to your Dashboard.

### Resetting Your Password

If you've forgotten your password:

1. Click the **Forgot Password** link on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Click the link in the email
5. Enter your **new password** (minimum 6 characters)
6. **Confirm your new password**
7. Click **Update Password**

You'll be redirected to the login page where you can sign in with your new password.

## Your Dashboard

### Overview

The Dashboard is your home base in the User Portal. When you log in, this is the first page you'll see. It gives you a quick overview of:

- **Your enrolled services** - All the services you've signed up for
- **Important notifications** - Things that need your attention
- **Quick stats** - A summary of your account status

### Notifications

At the top of your Dashboard, you may see notification messages. These alerts let you know about:

- **Incomplete profile** - If you haven't filled in your name or phone number
- **Missing billing address** - A reminder to add your billing information
- **No services enrolled** - Encouragement to browse and enroll in services

You can dismiss notifications by clicking the **X** button on each one.

### Quick Stats

Below the notifications, you'll see quick stat cards showing:

- **Active Services** - How many services you currently have active
- **Payments & Billing** - Your billing status
- **Paused Services** - How many services are currently paused (if any)

## Managing Services

### Browsing Available Services

To see what services are available:

1. Click **Enable Services** in the top navigation menu
2. Browse the list of available services
3. Each service card shows:
   - The service name and description
   - Features included with the service
   - Pricing information
   - Current status (Available, Active, or Paused)

### Enrolling in a Service

To enroll in a new service:

1. Find the service you want on the **Enable Services** page
2. Click the **Get Started** button
3. Complete the 4-step enrollment process:

**Step 1 - Read the Agreement**
- Scroll through and read the service agreement carefully
- Check the box confirming you've read and agree to the terms
- Click **Continue**

**Step 2 - Acknowledge Commitments**
- Review each commitment listed
- Check each box to acknowledge you understand
- Click **Continue**

**Step 3 - Connect Your Broker Account**
- Select your broker from the dropdown menu
- Enter your account number
- Enter your account password (click the eye icon to show/hide)
- Click **Continue**

**Step 4 - Confirm**
- Review all the information you entered
- Click **Confirm** to complete enrollment

After enrollment, you'll be taken back to your Dashboard with a success message.

### Viewing Service Details

To see details about a service you're enrolled in:

1. From your **Dashboard**, find the service card
2. Click **View Details** or **Manage Service**
3. You'll see:
   - Service status and enrollment date
   - Your signed agreement details
   - Your broker account information
   - Features included with the service

### Pausing a Service

If you need to temporarily pause a service:

1. Go to the service's detail page
2. Click the **Pause Service** button (yellow)
3. Optionally, enter a reason for pausing
4. Click **Yes, Pause** to confirm

Your service will be paused and your broker account will be temporarily deactivated. Your data will be preserved.

### Reactivating a Service

To reactivate a paused service:

1. Go to the paused service's detail page
2. Click the **Reactivate Service** button (green)
3. Click **Yes, Reactivate** to confirm

Your service and broker account will be active again.

### Removing a Service

To permanently remove a service:

1. Go to the service's detail page
2. Click the **Remove Service** button (red)
3. Read the warning message carefully
4. Click **Yes, Remove** to confirm

**Warning:** Removing a service is permanent. Your service agreement and broker account connection will be deleted and cannot be recovered.

## Your Profile

### Viewing Account Information

To view your account details:

1. Click **Profile** in the top navigation menu
2. The **Account Info** section shows:
   - Your name and avatar
   - Email address and verification status
   - Member since date
   - Last sign-in time
   - Your unique User ID

### Editing Your Profile

To update your personal information:

1. Go to the **Profile** page
2. In the **Edit Profile** section, you can update:
   - **First Name**
   - **Last Name**
   - **Country Code** (for your phone number)
   - **Phone Number** (10 digits, will be formatted automatically)
3. Click **Save Changes**

A green success message will appear when your changes are saved.

### Changing Your Password

To change your password:

1. Go to the **Profile** page
2. Find the **Change Password** section
3. Enter your **current password**
4. Enter your **new password** (minimum 6 characters)
5. **Confirm your new password**
6. Click **Update Password**

A success message will appear, and the password fields will be cleared.

## Payments & Billing

### Setting Up Your Billing Address

Before you can receive invoices, you need to add a billing address:

1. Click **Payments & Billing** in the navigation menu
2. Click the **Billing Address** tab
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Address Line 1** (required)
   - **Address Line 2** (optional)
   - **City** (required)
   - **State** (required)
   - **Postal Code** (required)
4. Click **Save**

A green checkmark will appear confirming your billing account is active.

### Viewing Invoices

To see your invoices:

1. Click **Payments & Billing** in the navigation menu
2. Click the **Invoices** tab
3. You'll see a list of all your invoices showing:
   - Invoice number
   - Amount
   - Status (Open or Paid)
   - Due date (for unpaid invoices)
   - Payment date (for paid invoices)

**Status Icons:**
- **Warning icon** - Invoice is unpaid/open
- **Checkmark icon** - Invoice has been paid

### Making a Payment

To pay an unpaid invoice:

1. Go to the **Invoices** tab on the Billing page
2. Find the invoice marked as "Open"
3. Click the **Pay** button or payment link
4. You'll be taken to a secure payment page
5. Complete your payment
6. You'll be redirected back with a success message

## Navigation Tips

### Top Navigation Bar

The navigation bar at the top of the screen includes:

- **Logo** - Click to return to the Dashboard
- **Dashboard** - Your home page with service overview
- **Enable Services** - Browse and enroll in services
- **Payments & Billing** - Manage billing and view invoices
- **Profile** - Update your personal information
- **Help** - Get additional support (opens external link)

### Account Menu

Click your **profile avatar** (circle with your initials) in the top right corner to:

- **Edit Profile** - Quick link to profile page
- **Sign Out** - Log out of your account

### Account Status Badge

Next to the navigation, you'll see your account status:

- **Approved** (green) - Your profile is complete
- **Pending** (yellow) - Please complete your profile information

### Mobile Navigation

On mobile devices:

1. Tap the **menu icon** (three horizontal lines) in the top left
2. A slide-out menu will appear with all navigation options
3. Tap any link to navigate
4. Tap the **X** to close the menu

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I verify my email address?**

A: When you sign up, a verification email is sent automatically. Click the link in that email to verify your account. If you didn't receive it, check your spam folder.

**Q: Can I enroll in multiple services?**

A: Yes! You can enroll in multiple services. Some services may have enrollment limits, which will be shown as "Limit Reached" if applicable.

**Q: What happens when I pause a service?**

A: When you pause a service, your broker account is temporarily deactivated, but all your data is preserved. You can reactivate at any time to resume the service.

**Q: Is removing a service permanent?**

A: Yes. When you remove a service, your service agreement and broker account connection are permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

**Q: How do I know if I have unpaid invoices?**

A: A warning badge will appear on the "Payments & Billing" link in the navigation if you have unpaid invoices.

**Q: Why can't I save my phone number?**

A: Phone numbers must be exactly 10 digits (numbers only). The system will automatically format it for you.

**Q: How do I change my email address?**

A: For security reasons, email addresses cannot be changed through the portal. Please contact support for assistance.
